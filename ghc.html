:: Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing 2015 ::

I had the greatest opportunity to attend Grace Hopper in October 2015 in Houston, Texas as the first female student from Georgia Gwinnett College. I had an article written about from The Science of Technology Newsletter called 'Eureka'.


If you are unaware what this conference is all about, this is a conference to celebrate Women Technologists from all over the world. I was able to attend Chelsea Clinton and Sheryl Sandberg talks. I also opportunities to interview with reputable companies such as: Capital One, Travelers, GoDaddy, and Intuit.


The Prezi Presentation to the left, was what I presented to other female students of the WIT@GGC Student Organization to encourage them to apply and attend.
